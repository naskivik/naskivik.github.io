Ronson Johnson 2

Release date: December 21 2019

Basic Info

After a year of development, the sequel is here. A new engine with 3 different weapons. A longer campaign and up to 30 minutes of gameplay, with an endless zombie mode.

Story

You are Ronson Johnson, relaxing after the aftermath in the first game.

You get a phone call from somebody giving you a mission. To take back stolen weapon plans and take down a commander in a group of terrorists.

Ronson Johnson ploughs through hundreds of enemies in order to get through the defenses in order to reacher the terrorist commander.

But in the way, there are multiple things to set him up, delaying his way through.

Controls

WASD to move, move mouse to aim, left click to shoot gun, right click to throw grenade, middle click for hellbender
